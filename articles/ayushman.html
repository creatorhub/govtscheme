Ayushman Bharat Yojana 2026

Introduction

Ayushman Bharat Yojana, also known as Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY), is one of the largest government-funded health insurance schemes in the world. The scheme was launched by the Government of India to provide free medical treatment and health insurance coverage to economically weaker families across the country.

Healthcare expenses can often become a financial burden for low-income families. Many families are unable to afford expensive medical treatments or hospital expenses. To address this issue and improve healthcare accessibility, the government launched the Ayushman Bharat scheme.

Under this scheme, eligible families receive health insurance coverage of up to ₹5 lakh per year. This coverage can be used for hospitalization, surgeries, and other medical treatments at empaneled hospitals across India.

Overview of Ayushman Bharat Scheme

The Ayushman Bharat scheme was launched in September 2018 with the aim of improving healthcare accessibility for millions of Indian citizens. The scheme focuses primarily on providing financial protection against major health expenses.

The scheme provides cashless treatment at government and private hospitals that are empaneled under the program. Beneficiaries do not need to pay money at the hospital as the government covers the treatment cost.

  • Health coverage: ₹5 lakh per family per year
  • Coverage type: Cashless treatment
  • Beneficiaries: Economically weaker families
  • Hospitals: Government and empaneled private hospitals
  • Launch year: 2018

Objectives of Ayushman Bharat Yojana

The main objective of the Ayushman Bharat scheme is to ensure that poor and vulnerable families receive quality healthcare services without facing financial hardship.

  • Provide free healthcare services to poor families
  • Reduce healthcare expenses for low-income households
  • Improve access to quality medical facilities
  • Encourage early treatment and diagnosis
  • Strengthen India's healthcare system

Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility for Ayushman Bharat Yojana is determined based on the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) data. Families listed in this database are eligible for benefits under the scheme.

  • Families listed in SECC database
  • Low-income or economically weaker families
  • Rural and urban poor households
  • Families without stable income sources

Eligible families do not need to pay any registration fee to enroll in the scheme.

Documents Required

Beneficiaries must provide certain documents when verifying eligibility or receiving treatment under the scheme.

  • Aadhar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Identity Proof
  • Family identification documents
  • Registered mobile number

Health Coverage and Treatments

The Ayushman Bharat scheme covers a wide range of medical treatments and healthcare services. Beneficiaries can receive treatment at both government and private hospitals that are part of the scheme.

The scheme covers hospital expenses including doctor fees, medicines, diagnostics, surgeries and post-treatment care.

  • Hospitalization expenses
  • Major surgeries
  • Cancer treatment
  • Heart surgery
  • Kidney treatment
  • Maternity services

Benefits of Ayushman Bharat Scheme

The scheme provides several benefits to eligible families and improves access to healthcare services.

  • ₹5 lakh annual health coverage
  • Cashless treatment at hospitals
  • No registration fee required
  • Covers major surgeries and treatments
  • Available across India

The scheme has helped millions of families receive medical treatment without worrying about financial expenses.

How to Check Eligibility

Beneficiaries can check their eligibility status through the official Ayushman Bharat website.

  1. Visit the official Ayushman Bharat portal.
  2. Enter your mobile number.
  3. Verify using OTP.
  4. Check your eligibility status.

How to Apply for Ayushman Bharat Yojana

Most beneficiaries are automatically selected based on SECC data. However, individuals can verify their eligibility and register at nearby health centers.

Steps to apply:

  1. Visit nearest Common Service Center (CSC).
  2. Provide identity and family details.
  3. Verify eligibility in the database.
  4. Receive Ayushman Bharat health card.

Ayushman Bharat Health Card

Eligible beneficiaries receive a health card which allows them to access treatment at empaneled hospitals.

The health card contains beneficiary information and enables cashless treatment under the scheme.

Impact of Ayushman Bharat Scheme

Since its launch, Ayushman Bharat has significantly improved access to healthcare services for millions of families. The scheme has reduced healthcare expenses and improved treatment accessibility in rural and urban areas.

Millions of treatments have already been provided under the scheme, making it one of the most successful healthcare programs in India.

Future of the Scheme

The government continues to expand the Ayushman Bharat scheme by adding more hospitals, improving digital systems and expanding coverage for beneficiaries.

The scheme is expected to play a major role in improving India's healthcare infrastructure in the coming years.

Conclusion

Ayushman Bharat Yojana is a revolutionary healthcare initiative launched by the Government of India. By providing financial protection against medical expenses, the scheme ensures that poor and vulnerable families receive the healthcare services they need.

The scheme has improved healthcare accessibility and reduced financial stress for millions of families across India.
