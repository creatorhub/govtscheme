Ayushman Bharat Yojana

Introduction

Ayushman Bharat Yojana is a health insurance scheme launched by the Government of India to provide free medical treatment to economically weaker families.

Overview

The scheme offers financial protection to families by covering hospital expenses and medical treatments.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must belong to eligible income group
  • Must be listed in government beneficiary database
  • Valid identity documents required

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Identity Proof

Benefits

Eligible families can receive free medical treatment at empaneled hospitals across India.

How to Apply

Beneficiaries can check eligibility and register through the official government portal or health centers.
