Ayushman Bharat Yojana 2026

Published: 2026

Introduction

Ayushman Bharat Yojana, officially known as Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY), is one of the largest government-funded health insurance programs in the world. The scheme was launched by the Government of India to provide free healthcare services and financial protection to economically weaker families across the country.

Healthcare expenses can often become a serious financial burden for many families. Medical treatments, surgeries and hospitalization costs can be extremely expensive. To address this issue, the Indian government introduced the Ayushman Bharat scheme to ensure that poor families receive quality healthcare without worrying about high medical costs.

Overview of Ayushman Bharat Scheme

The Ayushman Bharat scheme was launched in September 2018. Under this program, eligible families receive health insurance coverage of up to ₹5 lakh per year for medical treatments and hospitalization.

The scheme provides cashless treatment at government and private hospitals that are empaneled under the program. Beneficiaries do not need to pay hospital bills directly because the treatment cost is covered by the government.

  • Health coverage: ₹5 lakh per family per year
  • Treatment type: Cashless hospitalization
  • Beneficiaries: Economically weaker families
  • Hospitals: Government and empaneled private hospitals
  • Scheme launched: 2018

Objectives of Ayushman Bharat Yojana

The primary objective of the Ayushman Bharat scheme is to improve access to healthcare services for low-income families and protect them from financial hardship caused by medical expenses.

  • Provide free healthcare services to poor families
  • Reduce financial burden caused by medical treatments
  • Improve access to quality hospitals and healthcare facilities
  • Encourage early diagnosis and treatment
  • Strengthen the healthcare system in India

Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility for the Ayushman Bharat scheme is determined based on the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) data. Families listed in this database are eligible for the scheme.

  • Families listed in the SECC database
  • Low-income or economically weaker families
  • Rural and urban poor households
  • Families without stable sources of income

Eligible families do not need to pay any registration fee to receive benefits under the scheme.

Documents Required

Beneficiaries must provide certain documents when verifying their eligibility or receiving treatment at hospitals.

  • Aadhar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Identity Proof
  • Family Identification Documents
  • Registered Mobile Number

Medical Treatments Covered

The Ayushman Bharat scheme covers a wide range of healthcare services and treatments. Beneficiaries can receive treatment at both government and private hospitals that are part of the scheme.

  • Hospitalization expenses
  • Major surgeries
  • Cancer treatments
  • Heart surgeries
  • Kidney treatments
  • Maternity services
  • Emergency medical treatments

Benefits of Ayushman Bharat Scheme

The scheme provides several benefits that significantly improve healthcare access for economically weaker families.

  • Health insurance coverage of ₹5 lakh per year
  • Cashless treatment at empaneled hospitals
  • No registration fee required
  • Coverage for major medical treatments and surgeries
  • Access to hospitals across India

How to Check Eligibility

Citizens can check their eligibility for the Ayushman Bharat scheme through the official government portal.

  1. Visit the official Ayushman Bharat website.
  2. Enter your registered mobile number.
  3. Verify the OTP received on your phone.
  4. Check your eligibility status.

How to Apply for Ayushman Bharat Yojana

Most beneficiaries are automatically selected based on the SECC database. However, individuals can visit nearby health centers to verify eligibility.

  1. Visit the nearest Common Service Center (CSC).
  2. Provide identity and family details.
  3. Verify eligibility in the database.
  4. Receive the Ayushman Bharat health card.

Ayushman Bharat Health Card

Eligible beneficiaries receive a health card that allows them to access treatment at empaneled hospitals. The card contains important details of the beneficiary and enables cashless treatment.

Impact of Ayushman Bharat Scheme

Since its launch, the Ayushman Bharat scheme has helped millions of families receive medical treatment without financial stress. The program has significantly improved healthcare accessibility in rural and urban areas.

Thousands of hospitals across India are now part of the scheme, providing better healthcare services for eligible citizens.

Future of Ayushman Bharat Scheme

The government continues to expand the scheme by adding more hospitals, improving digital healthcare systems and expanding coverage for beneficiaries.

The program is expected to play a major role in strengthening India's healthcare infrastructure in the coming years.
