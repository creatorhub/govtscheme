National Scholarship Program

Introduction

The National Scholarship Program is a government initiative that provides financial support to students from economically weaker backgrounds to help them continue their education.

Overview

The scholarship aims to support students studying in schools, colleges and universities. The program helps students cover educational expenses such as tuition fees and study materials.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen
  • Student must be enrolled in a recognized educational institution
  • Family income must fall under the eligible income limit

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Educational Certificates
  • Bank Account Details

Benefits

Students receive financial assistance that can help cover education costs and support their academic journey.

How to Apply

Students can apply online through the official National Scholarship Portal by filling out the application form and submitting required documents.
