Railway NTPC Admit Card

Introduction

Railway NTPC Admit Card is a mandatory document required to appear for the NTPC examination conducted by Indian Railways.

Overview

Candidates who apply for Railway NTPC recruitment can download their admit card from the official railway recruitment portal before the exam.

Details Mentioned on Admit Card

  • Candidate Name
  • Roll Number
  • Exam Date and Time
  • Exam Center Address
  • Exam Instructions

Documents Required with Admit Card

  • Printed Admit Card
  • Valid Identity Proof
  • Passport Size Photograph

How to Download Admit Card

Candidates can download the admit card from the official Railway Recruitment Board website by entering their registration number and date of birth.
