Minority Scholarship

Introduction

Minority Scholarship is a government initiative designed to provide financial assistance to students belonging to minority communities so they can continue their education.

Overview

This scholarship program helps students from minority communities pursue education at school, college and university levels.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must belong to a recognized minority community
  • Student must be enrolled in a recognized institution
  • Family income must fall under the eligibility limit

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Minority Certificate
  • Income Certificate
  • Educational Certificates
  • Bank Account Details

Benefits

The scholarship provides financial assistance to help students cover tuition fees, books and educational expenses.

How to Apply

Students can apply through the official National Scholarship Portal and submit required documents for verification.
