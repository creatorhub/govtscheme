PM Jan Dhan Yojana

Introduction

PM Jan Dhan Yojana is a financial inclusion scheme that helps people open bank accounts.

Overview

The scheme provides banking facilities to people who previously had no access to financial services.

Benefits

  • Zero balance bank account
  • Insurance benefits
  • Easy access to financial services

How to Apply

Applicants can visit nearby banks to open a Jan Dhan account.
