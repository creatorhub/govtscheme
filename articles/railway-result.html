Railway NTPC Result 2026

Published: 2026

Introduction

Railway NTPC Result is released by the Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) after the completion of the NTPC examination. The result determines which candidates have qualified for the next stage of the recruitment process.

The Railway NTPC exam is one of the most popular government exams in India. Thousands of candidates participate in this exam every year to secure jobs in Indian Railways.

Candidates can check their results online through the official Railway Recruitment Board website.

Overview of Railway NTPC Result

  • Exam Name: Railway NTPC Examination
  • Conducting Authority: Railway Recruitment Board (RRB)
  • Result Mode: Online
  • Selection Process: Multi-stage examination

Stages of Railway NTPC Selection Process

  • Computer Based Test (CBT 1)
  • Computer Based Test (CBT 2)
  • Skill Test or Aptitude Test
  • Document Verification
  • Medical Examination

Details Mentioned in Result

  • Candidate Name
  • Roll Number
  • Registration Number
  • Marks Obtained
  • Qualification Status
  • Category Rank

How to Check Railway NTPC Result

  1. Visit official Railway Recruitment Board website
  2. Click on NTPC Result link
  3. Select your RRB region
  4. Download result PDF
  5. Search your roll number

Railway NTPC Cut Off Marks

Cut off marks represent the minimum score required to qualify for the next stage of the recruitment process.

  • Exam difficulty level
  • Total candidates appearing
  • Available vacancies
  • Candidate category

Railway NTPC Merit List

The merit list includes the roll numbers of candidates who scored the highest marks in the examination. Candidates included in the merit list move to the next stage of recruitment.

What to Do After Result

Candidates who qualify should prepare for the next stage such as skill test or document verification. Candidates should regularly check the official website for updates.

Benefits of Railway NTPC Jobs

  • Stable government job
  • Regular salary and allowances
  • Pension benefits
  • Career promotion opportunities
  • Travel and medical benefits

Conclusion

Railway NTPC Result is an important milestone in the recruitment process for Indian Railways jobs. Candidates should check their results through official websites and prepare for the next stage accordingly.
