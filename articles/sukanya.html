Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

Introduction

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana is a government savings scheme designed to secure the future of girl children in India.

Overview

This scheme allows parents to open a savings account for their daughter and deposit money regularly.

Eligibility

  • Girl child must be below 10 years
  • Account must be opened by parents or guardians

Benefits

The scheme provides attractive interest rates and tax benefits.
