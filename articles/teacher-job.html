Teacher Recruitment

Introduction

Teacher Recruitment is conducted by state and central education departments to hire qualified candidates for teaching positions in schools and colleges.

Overview

Teaching jobs are highly respected and provide stable career opportunities. Recruitment usually happens through exams such as TET, CTET and state teacher recruitment exams.

Eligibility

  • Candidate must be an Indian citizen
  • Minimum qualification: Graduation with B.Ed or equivalent degree
  • Age limit generally between 21 and 40 years depending on the state

Selection Process

  • Written Examination
  • Teaching Eligibility Test
  • Interview
  • Document Verification

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Educational Certificates
  • B.Ed Certificate
  • Passport Size Photograph

How to Apply

Candidates can apply through the official state education department website when teacher recruitment notifications are released.
