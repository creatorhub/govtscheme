Railway Recruitment 2026

Introduction

Railway Recruitment 2026 is one of the most awaited job opportunities for candidates who want to work in the Indian railway sector. Every year the railway department releases various job vacancies for different posts.

Overview

Indian Railways offers employment opportunities in technical and non-technical positions. Candidates from different educational backgrounds can apply for railway jobs.

Eligibility

  • Candidate must be an Indian citizen
  • Minimum qualification: 10th / 12th / Graduation depending on the post
  • Age limit generally between 18 and 35 years

Selection Process

  • Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Physical Efficiency Test (for some posts)
  • Document Verification

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Educational Certificates
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Identity Proof

How to Apply

Candidates can apply online through the official Indian Railways recruitment portal. Applicants must fill the application form and upload required documents.
