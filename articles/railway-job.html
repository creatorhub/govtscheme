Railway Recruitment 2026

Published: 2026

Introduction

Railway Recruitment 2026 is one of the most awaited government job opportunities for candidates who want to build a career in the Indian Railways sector. Indian Railways is one of the largest employers in the world and provides thousands of employment opportunities every year.

Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) releases notifications for different posts such as NTPC, Group D, Assistant Loco Pilot, Junior Engineer and other technical or non-technical positions.

Overview of Railway Recruitment

  • Recruitment Authority: Railway Recruitment Board (RRB)
  • Sector: Indian Railways
  • Job Type: Government Job
  • Application Mode: Online Application

Popular Railway Job Posts

  • Railway NTPC Posts
  • Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP)
  • Railway Group D
  • Junior Engineer (JE)
  • Station Master
  • Ticket Collector

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidate must be an Indian citizen
  • Qualification depends on post
  • Age limit generally between 18 – 35 years
  • Candidate must meet medical standards

Educational Qualification

  • Group D – 10th pass or ITI
  • NTPC – 12th pass or Graduation
  • Technical Posts – Diploma / Engineering

Age Limit

The age limit varies depending on the recruitment post. Age relaxation is provided for reserved categories according to government rules.

Selection Process

  • Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Skill Test / Physical Test
  • Document Verification
  • Medical Examination

Computer Based Test

The CBT exam usually includes questions related to General Knowledge, Mathematics, Reasoning and Current Affairs.

Physical Efficiency Test

For certain posts such as Group D, candidates must pass a physical test that evaluates their physical strength and endurance.

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Educational Certificates
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof

How to Apply for Railway Recruitment

  1. Visit official RRB website
  2. Create registration account
  3. Fill online application form
  4. Upload documents
  5. Submit application before deadline

Benefits of Railway Jobs

  • Stable government employment
  • Regular salary and allowances
  • Pension benefits
  • Career growth opportunities
  • Medical and travel benefits

Career Growth in Railways

Employees in Indian Railways receive multiple opportunities for promotions. With experience and performance, employees can move to higher administrative positions.

Conclusion

Railway Recruitment 2026 provides excellent career opportunities for candidates seeking government jobs. With proper preparation and dedication, candidates can successfully secure a position in Indian Railways.
