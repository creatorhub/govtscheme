Police Recruitment

Introduction

Police Recruitment is conducted by various state police departments and central agencies to recruit candidates for maintaining law and order. Many candidates prepare every year to join the police force.

Overview

Police jobs include positions such as constable, sub inspector and other law enforcement roles. These jobs offer stability, respect and opportunities for career growth.

Eligibility

  • Candidate must be an Indian citizen
  • Minimum qualification usually 10th or 12th pass depending on the post
  • Age limit generally between 18 and 30 years

Selection Process

  • Written Examination
  • Physical Efficiency Test
  • Medical Examination
  • Document Verification

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Educational Certificates
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Identity Proof

How to Apply

Candidates can apply through the official state police recruitment website when the notification is released.
