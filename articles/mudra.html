Mudra Loan Scheme

Introduction

Mudra Loan Scheme is a government initiative designed to support small businesses and entrepreneurs by providing financial assistance.

Overview

The scheme helps small business owners access loans without collateral and encourages entrepreneurship in India.

Eligibility

  • Small business owners
  • Entrepreneurs starting new businesses
  • Self-employed individuals

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Business Plan
  • Bank Account Details

Benefits

Loans are provided to help small businesses grow and create employment opportunities.

How to Apply

Applicants can apply through banks and financial institutions offering Mudra loans.
