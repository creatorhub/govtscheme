PM Kisan Yojana 2026

Published: 2026

PM Kisan Yojana, officially known as Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN), is one of the most important agricultural welfare schemes launched by the Government of India. The main objective of this scheme is to provide financial assistance to farmers so they can manage agricultural expenses and improve their standard of living.

Agriculture is the backbone of India's economy. Millions of farmers depend on agriculture for their livelihood. However, many farmers face financial challenges due to rising costs of farming equipment, fertilizers, seeds, irrigation and other essential resources. To address these challenges, the government launched the PM Kisan scheme.

Overview

The PM Kisan scheme was launched in February 2019 by the Government of India. Under this scheme, eligible farmers receive financial support of ₹6000 per year. The amount is transferred directly to farmers' bank accounts in three installments of ₹2000 each.

The scheme uses the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) system, which ensures that money is transferred directly to farmers without middlemen. This helps improve transparency and prevents corruption.

  • Total yearly support: ₹6000
  • Three installments of ₹2000
  • Direct bank transfer
  • Available across India

Objectives of PM Kisan Scheme

The main objectives of the PM Kisan scheme include improving farmers' financial stability and supporting agricultural productivity.

  • Provide direct financial assistance to farmers
  • Support farmers in purchasing seeds and fertilizers
  • Improve agricultural productivity
  • Reduce financial stress among farmers
  • Encourage modern farming practices

Eligibility

To receive benefits under the PM Kisan scheme, farmers must meet certain eligibility criteria defined by the government.

  • The applicant must be an Indian citizen.
  • The applicant must be a farmer.
  • The farmer must own cultivable agricultural land.
  • Valid identity proof must be provided.

However, certain individuals are not eligible for the scheme. These include income taxpayers, government employees, and institutional landholders.

Documents Required

Farmers must provide the following documents when applying for PM Kisan Yojana:

  • Aadhar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Land Ownership Documents
  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof

Benefits

PM Kisan Yojana offers several benefits to farmers. The scheme helps farmers manage agricultural expenses and improves their financial security.

  • Direct financial assistance to farmers
  • Improved agricultural productivity
  • Better financial security for farmers
  • Reduced dependency on loans
  • Support during difficult agricultural seasons

How to Apply

Farmers can apply for PM Kisan Yojana online through the official website.

  1. Visit the official PM Kisan website.
  2. Click on "New Farmer Registration".
  3. Enter Aadhaar number and personal details.
  4. Fill the application form.
  5. Submit the form and wait for verification.

Installment Details

The government releases the financial assistance in three installments each year.

  • First installment: April to July
  • Second installment: August to November
  • Third installment: December to March

How to Check Beneficiary Status

Farmers can check their PM Kisan beneficiary status online.

  1. Visit the official PM Kisan portal.
  2. Click on "Beneficiary Status".
  3. Enter Aadhaar number or registration number.
  4. Submit to view status.

Common Problems Faced by Farmers

Sometimes farmers face issues such as incorrect bank details, Aadhaar mismatch, or land record verification problems. These issues can delay payments.

Farmers are advised to ensure that all information provided during registration is accurate to avoid delays in receiving benefits.

Impact of PM Kisan Scheme

Since its launch, the PM Kisan scheme has benefited millions of farmers across India. The scheme has helped farmers improve their financial stability and invest more in agricultural activities.

Many farmers have used the financial assistance to buy seeds, fertilizers, irrigation equipment and other essential farming tools.

Future of PM Kisan Scheme

The government continues to improve the PM Kisan scheme by expanding coverage and improving transparency. Digital verification systems and improved land records are helping ensure that benefits reach the correct beneficiaries.

Conclusion

PM Kisan Yojana is one of the most impactful farmer welfare schemes launched by the Government of India. By providing direct financial assistance to farmers, the scheme helps improve agricultural productivity and ensures better financial security for rural families.
