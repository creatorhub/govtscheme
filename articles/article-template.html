PM Kisan Yojana 2026

Published: 2026

Introduction

PM Kisan Yojana, also known as Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN), is one of the largest farmer welfare schemes launched by the Government of India. The scheme provides financial assistance to small and marginal farmers across the country.

Agriculture is a major source of livelihood for millions of people in India. However, farmers often face financial challenges such as rising costs of seeds, fertilizers, irrigation and farming equipment. To support farmers and strengthen the agricultural sector, the government launched the PM Kisan scheme.

Overview of PM Kisan Scheme

The PM Kisan scheme was launched in February 2019. Under this scheme, eligible farmers receive financial assistance of ₹6000 per year. The amount is transferred directly to farmers' bank accounts through the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) system.

  • Total financial support: ₹6000 per year
  • Installments: Three installments of ₹2000
  • Payment mode: Direct bank transfer
  • Coverage: Farmers across India

Objectives of PM Kisan Scheme

The main objective of the PM Kisan scheme is to provide income support to farmers so they can manage agricultural expenses and improve productivity.

  • Provide direct income support to farmers
  • Improve agricultural productivity
  • Reduce financial stress in rural areas
  • Encourage modern farming techniques
  • Strengthen rural economy

Eligibility Criteria

Farmers must meet certain eligibility requirements to receive benefits under the PM Kisan scheme.

  • The applicant must be an Indian citizen
  • The applicant must be a farmer
  • The farmer must own cultivable agricultural land
  • Valid identity documents must be provided

Documents Required

Farmers must provide the following documents while applying for PM Kisan Yojana.

  • Aadhar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Land Ownership Documents
  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof

Benefits of PM Kisan Scheme

The scheme provides several benefits for farmers and helps improve their financial stability.

  • Direct financial support for farmers
  • Improved access to farming resources
  • Reduced dependency on agricultural loans
  • Better financial security for farmers
  • Support during difficult agricultural seasons

How to Apply for PM Kisan Yojana

Farmers can apply for PM Kisan Yojana through the official government portal.

  1. Visit the official PM Kisan website
  2. Click on “New Farmer Registration”
  3. Enter Aadhaar number and personal details
  4. Fill the registration form
  5. Submit the application

PM Kisan Installment Schedule

The financial assistance under the PM Kisan scheme is released in three installments every year.

  • First Installment – April to July
  • Second Installment – August to November
  • Third Installment – December to March

How to Check PM Kisan Beneficiary Status

Farmers can easily check their PM Kisan beneficiary status online.

  1. Visit the official PM Kisan portal
  2. Click on Beneficiary Status
  3. Enter Aadhaar number or registration number
  4. Click submit to view the status

Common Issues Faced by Farmers

Sometimes farmers face issues while receiving payments under the scheme. These issues may include incorrect Aadhaar details, bank account mismatch, or land verification problems.

Farmers should ensure that all information provided during registration is correct to avoid delays.

Impact of PM Kisan Scheme

Since its launch, the PM Kisan scheme has benefited millions of farmers across India. The scheme has helped farmers improve agricultural productivity and manage farming expenses more effectively.

Many farmers have used the financial support to purchase seeds, fertilizers and modern farming equipment.

Future of PM Kisan Scheme

The government continues to improve the PM Kisan scheme by introducing digital verification systems and better land record management.

These improvements help ensure that benefits reach the correct farmers and improve transparency in the system.
