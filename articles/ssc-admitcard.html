SSC CGL Admit Card

Introduction

SSC CGL Admit Card is an important document that candidates must carry to the examination center. It contains important details such as exam date, time and exam center.

Overview

The Staff Selection Commission releases the admit card for candidates who successfully submit the application form for the SSC CGL exam.

Details Mentioned on Admit Card

  • Candidate Name
  • Roll Number
  • Exam Date
  • Exam Center Address
  • Exam Instructions

Documents Required with Admit Card

  • Admit Card Printout
  • Valid Identity Proof
  • Passport Size Photograph

How to Download Admit Card

Candidates can download their admit card by visiting the official SSC website and entering their registration details.
