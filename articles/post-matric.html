Post Matric Scholarship

Introduction

Post Matric Scholarship is a government scholarship program designed to support students who continue their education after completing class 10.

Overview

The scholarship helps students from financially weaker families pursue higher education without financial difficulties.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen
  • Student must be studying after class 10
  • Family income must fall under the eligibility limit

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Educational Certificates
  • Bank Account Details

Benefits

The scholarship provides financial assistance for tuition fees, books and other educational expenses.

How to Apply

Students can apply online through the official scholarship portal and submit required documents for verification.
