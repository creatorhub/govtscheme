TET Admit Card

Introduction

TET Admit Card is an important document required for candidates appearing in the Teacher Eligibility Test examination.

Overview

Candidates who apply for the Teacher Eligibility Test can download their admit card from the official education department website before the exam date.

Details Mentioned on Admit Card

  • Candidate Name
  • Roll Number
  • Exam Date and Time
  • Exam Center Address
  • Important Instructions

Documents Required with Admit Card

  • Printed Admit Card
  • Valid Identity Proof
  • Passport Size Photograph

How to Download Admit Card

Candidates can download their admit card by visiting the official TET exam website and entering their registration details.
