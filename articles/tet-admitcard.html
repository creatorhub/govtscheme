TET Admit Card

Introduction

TET Admit Card is an important document required for candidates appearing in the Teacher Eligibility Test examination. The admit card acts as an official entry pass that allows candidates to enter the examination hall.

Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) is conducted by central and state education boards to determine the eligibility of candidates who want to become teachers in government schools. Candidates must carry their admit card along with valid identification proof on the exam day.

Overview of TET Admit Card

The admit card is released a few days before the examination date on the official website of the conducting authority. Candidates who have successfully submitted their application forms can download the admit card online.

  • Exam Name: Teacher Eligibility Test (TET)
  • Conducting Authority: State Education Board / Central Board
  • Admit Card Mode: Online
  • Exam Type: Eligibility Examination

Importance of Admit Card

The admit card contains important information about the candidate and examination schedule. Without the admit card, candidates are not allowed to appear in the examination under any circumstances.

Candidates should download the admit card as soon as it becomes available and verify all the details mentioned on it.

Details Mentioned on Admit Card

  • Candidate Name
  • Roll Number
  • Registration Number
  • Exam Date and Time
  • Exam Center Address
  • Important Instructions

Documents Required with Admit Card

  • Printed Admit Card
  • Valid Identity Proof (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID)
  • Passport Size Photograph

How to Download TET Admit Card

Candidates can download their admit card by following these steps:

  1. Visit the official TET exam website
  2. Click on the TET Admit Card link
  3. Enter registration number and date of birth
  4. Click submit to view the admit card
  5. Download and print the admit card

Important Instructions for Candidates

Candidates should carefully read all the instructions mentioned on the admit card before appearing for the examination.

  • Reach the exam center before reporting time
  • Carry required documents
  • Avoid carrying prohibited electronic devices
  • Follow exam guidelines provided by authorities

Conclusion

TET Admit Card is an essential document for candidates appearing in the Teacher Eligibility Test. Candidates should download their admit card from the official website and verify all details before the examination day.

Proper preparation and following exam instructions will help candidates successfully appear in the examination.
