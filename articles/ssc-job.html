SSC Recruitment 2026

Introduction

SSC Recruitment is conducted by the Staff Selection Commission to fill various government job positions in different departments of India.

Overview

Every year SSC releases multiple recruitment notifications such as SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, SSC MTS and other exams for candidates seeking government jobs.

Eligibility

  • Candidate must be an Indian citizen
  • Minimum qualification: 12th pass or graduation depending on exam
  • Age limit usually between 18 and 32 years

Selection Process

  • Computer Based Examination
  • Skill Test or Typing Test
  • Document Verification

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Educational Certificates
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Identity Proof

How to Apply

Candidates can apply online through the official SSC website by filling out the application form and uploading the required documents.
