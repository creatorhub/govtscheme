PM Kisan Yojana

Introduction

PM Kisan Yojana is a government scheme launched to provide financial assistance to farmers in India. Under this scheme eligible farmers receive financial support directly in their bank accounts.

Overview

The scheme was launched by the Government of India to support small and marginal farmers. Farmers receive financial assistance in installments every year which helps them manage agricultural expenses.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be an Indian farmer
  • Must own agricultural land
  • Valid identity documents required

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Land Ownership Documents

Benefits

Farmers receive financial assistance directly in their bank accounts. This helps them buy seeds, fertilizers and manage farming expenses.

How to Apply

Farmers can apply online through the official government portal or through local agriculture offices.
