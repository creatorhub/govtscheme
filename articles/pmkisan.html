PM Kisan Yojana 2026

The PM Kisan Yojana, officially known as the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN), is one of the largest farmer welfare schemes launched by the Government of India. The primary goal of the scheme is to provide financial assistance to farmers across the country so they can manage agricultural expenses and improve productivity.

Millions of farmers in India depend on agriculture for their livelihood. However, due to rising costs of seeds, fertilizers, equipment, irrigation and labor, farmers often struggle to maintain stable incomes. To address these challenges, the government introduced the PM Kisan Samman Nidhi scheme in 2019.

Under this scheme, eligible farmers receive financial assistance directly into their bank accounts through the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) system. This ensures transparency and eliminates middlemen in the distribution of funds.

Introduction to PM Kisan Yojana

Agriculture is the backbone of the Indian economy. A large portion of the population lives in rural areas and relies heavily on farming activities for income. Farmers often face financial stress due to unpredictable weather conditions, crop failures and fluctuating market prices.

To support farmers and strengthen the agricultural sector, the Government of India launched the PM Kisan scheme. The scheme aims to provide minimum income support to farmers so they can meet their agricultural needs and improve their standard of living.

Since its launch, the scheme has benefited millions of farmers across the country. The government continues to expand and improve the scheme to reach more beneficiaries.

Overview of PM Kisan Scheme

The PM Kisan Yojana provides direct income support to farmers. The government transfers financial assistance directly to the bank accounts of eligible farmers in three installments each year.

  • Total financial assistance: ₹6000 per year
  • Installments: Three installments of ₹2000 each
  • Payment method: Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
  • Beneficiaries: Small and marginal farmers
  • Launch year: 2019

The funds provided under the scheme help farmers purchase agricultural inputs such as seeds, fertilizers, pesticides and farming equipment.

Objectives of PM Kisan Yojana

The main objective of the PM Kisan scheme is to support farmers financially and strengthen the agricultural sector. The scheme aims to improve the economic condition of farmers and ensure sustainable farming practices.

  • Provide financial support to farmers
  • Improve agricultural productivity
  • Reduce financial stress among farmers
  • Encourage modern farming techniques
  • Promote rural economic development

Eligibility Criteria

Farmers who wish to receive benefits under the PM Kisan scheme must meet certain eligibility conditions defined by the government.

  • The applicant must be an Indian citizen.
  • The applicant must be a farmer.
  • The farmer must own cultivable agricultural land.
  • Valid identity documents must be provided.

However, certain categories of individuals are not eligible for the scheme, including income taxpayers, government employees and institutional landholders.

Documents Required

To apply for the PM Kisan scheme, farmers must submit certain documents for verification.

  • Aadhar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Land Ownership Documents
  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof

Benefits of PM Kisan Scheme

The PM Kisan Yojana provides several benefits to farmers. The financial assistance helps farmers manage agricultural expenses and improves their financial stability.

  • Direct financial assistance to farmers
  • Money transferred directly to bank accounts
  • Helps farmers buy seeds and fertilizers
  • Supports farmers during difficult times
  • Improves agricultural productivity

How to Apply for PM Kisan Yojana

Farmers can apply for the scheme through both online and offline methods. The government has made the registration process simple and accessible.

  1. Visit the official PM Kisan website.
  2. Select the "New Farmer Registration" option.
  3. Enter Aadhaar number and personal details.
  4. Fill the application form.
  5. Submit the form and wait for verification.

PM Kisan Installment Schedule

The government releases three installments every year under the PM Kisan scheme.

  • First Installment: April – July
  • Second Installment: August – November
  • Third Installment: December – March

How to Check PM Kisan Beneficiary Status

Farmers can easily check their beneficiary status online through the official PM Kisan portal.

  1. Visit the official PM Kisan website.
  2. Click on "Beneficiary Status".
  3. Enter Aadhaar number or registration number.
  4. Click submit to view details.

Common Issues Faced by Farmers

Some farmers face issues such as incorrect bank details, Aadhaar verification problems or incomplete land records. These issues can delay payments. Farmers are advised to regularly update their details on the official portal.

Future of PM Kisan Scheme

The government is continuously working to improve the PM Kisan scheme by expanding coverage and improving transparency. Digital verification systems and improved land record management are helping ensure that benefits reach the correct beneficiaries.

Conclusion

PM Kisan Yojana is a major initiative by the Government of India to support farmers financially. By providing direct income support, the scheme helps farmers manage agricultural expenses and improve their livelihoods. The scheme has already benefited millions of farmers and continues to play an important role in strengthening India's agricultural economy.
