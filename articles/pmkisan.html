PM Kisan Yojana

PM Kisan Yojana is a government scheme that provides financial assistance to farmers in India.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be a farmer
  • Must have agricultural land
  • Valid identification documents required

Benefits

Farmers receive financial support directly in their bank accounts.

How to Apply

Applicants can apply through the official government portal or local agriculture office.
