Bank PO Recruitment

Introduction

Bank PO Recruitment is conducted by various banks in India to recruit candidates for the position of Probationary Officer. It is one of the most popular government job opportunities in the banking sector.

Overview

Candidates selected for the Bank PO position are responsible for managing banking operations, customer services, and financial transactions.

Eligibility

  • Candidate must be an Indian citizen
  • Minimum qualification: Graduation from a recognized university
  • Age limit usually between 20 and 30 years

Selection Process

  • Preliminary Examination
  • Main Examination
  • Interview

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Educational Certificates
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Identity Proof

How to Apply

Candidates can apply online through the official bank recruitment portals or through the banking examination authority website.
