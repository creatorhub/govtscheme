Bank PO Recruitment 2026

Published: 2026

Introduction

Bank PO Recruitment is one of the most popular recruitment processes in the Indian banking sector. Every year thousands of candidates apply for the Probationary Officer (PO) position in public sector banks. The recruitment process is conducted by organizations such as IBPS and SBI.

The position of Probationary Officer is considered a prestigious government job in India. It offers job stability, attractive salary packages and long term career growth opportunities.

Bank PO officers are responsible for managing daily banking operations, assisting customers, supervising financial transactions and ensuring smooth banking services.

Overview

  • Job Position: Probationary Officer (PO)
  • Sector: Banking
  • Recruitment Authority: IBPS / SBI / Public Sector Banks
  • Application Mode: Online
  • Selection Process: Exam + Interview

Candidates who successfully clear all stages of the recruitment process are appointed as Probationary Officers in various banks.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates applying for Bank PO recruitment must meet certain eligibility requirements set by the recruitment authority.

  • Candidate must be an Indian citizen.
  • Minimum qualification: Graduation from a recognized university.
  • Age limit usually between 20 and 30 years.
  • Age relaxation is available for reserved categories.

Educational Qualification

Applicants must have a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university. Some recruitment notifications may allow final year students to apply.

Age Limit

The general age limit for Bank PO recruitment is between 20 and 30 years. Age relaxation is provided for candidates belonging to reserved categories.

  • OBC Candidates: 3 years relaxation
  • SC/ST Candidates: 5 years relaxation
  • PwD Candidates: 10 years relaxation

Selection Process

The Bank PO recruitment process consists of three major stages.

  • Preliminary Examination
  • Main Examination
  • Interview

Preliminary Examination

The preliminary exam is the first stage of the selection process and tests the candidate's basic aptitude.

  • English Language
  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • Reasoning Ability

Main Examination

The main exam tests advanced knowledge and analytical abilities of the candidates.

  • Reasoning and Computer Aptitude
  • Banking Awareness
  • English Language
  • Data Analysis and Interpretation

Interview

Candidates who qualify in the main exam are invited for an interview. The interview assesses communication skills, personality and professional knowledge.

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Educational Certificates
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Identity Proof
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Salary and Benefits

Bank PO officers receive attractive salary packages along with several benefits.

  • Basic Salary
  • Dearness Allowance
  • House Rent Allowance
  • Travel Allowance
  • Medical Benefits

Apart from salary, officers also receive promotion opportunities and career growth in the banking sector.

How to Apply

Candidates can apply online through the official banking recruitment portals.

  1. Visit the official recruitment website.
  2. Register using email and mobile number.
  3. Fill the application form.
  4. Upload documents.
  5. Pay the application fee.
  6. Submit the form.

Preparation Tips

  • Practice previous year question papers
  • Take regular mock tests
  • Improve time management
  • Stay updated with banking awareness

Conclusion

Bank PO Recruitment offers excellent career opportunities for candidates who want to work in the banking sector. With proper preparation and dedication candidates can successfully clear the exam and secure a prestigious government job.
