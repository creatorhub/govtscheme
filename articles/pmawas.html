PM Awas Yojana

Introduction

PM Awas Yojana is a government housing scheme launched to provide affordable homes for economically weaker sections and low-income families in India.

Overview

The scheme aims to ensure housing for all by helping eligible families build or purchase homes with government assistance.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen
  • Family should not own a permanent house
  • Must belong to eligible income category

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Address Proof
  • Bank Account Details

Benefits

The scheme provides financial support and subsidies to help families build or buy their own homes.

How to Apply

Applicants can apply online through the official government portal or visit local government offices for assistance.
