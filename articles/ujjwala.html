Ujjwala Yojana

Introduction

Ujjwala Yojana is a government scheme that provides free LPG gas connections to families in India.

Overview

The scheme aims to promote clean cooking fuel and reduce the use of traditional fuels such as firewood and coal.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must belong to eligible household
  • Must have valid identity documents
  • Must not already have LPG connection

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Address Proof
  • Bank Account Details

Benefits

Families receive LPG connection and support for cleaner cooking methods.

How to Apply

Applicants can apply through authorized LPG distributors or government portals.
