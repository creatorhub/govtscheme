Government Clerk Jobs

Introduction

Government Clerk jobs are popular government positions that involve office administration and record management. Many candidates apply every year for clerk positions in different government departments.

Overview

Clerk jobs are available in various departments such as railways, banking, state government offices and public sector organizations.

Eligibility

  • Candidate must be an Indian citizen
  • Minimum qualification usually 12th pass or graduation
  • Age limit generally between 18 and 35 years

Selection Process

  • Written Examination
  • Typing Test
  • Document Verification

Documents Required

  • Aadhar Card
  • Educational Certificates
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Identity Proof

How to Apply

Candidates can apply through the official recruitment portals of government departments when clerk job notifications are released.
